A 4 Step-Approach to Next-Generation Data Classification

Learn how to revolutionize data discovery & data classification with a ML-driven approach, designed for today’s data environment.

Traditional approaches to classification aren’t able to address the privacy and security challenges of today’s data environment.

That’s why BigID approaches classification differently: not based on what’s worked in the past, or identifying what’s covered by specific legislation, but with an ML-based approach designed from the ground up.

With BigID, organizations can automatically classify data by person, type, category, attributes, and more - and automatically classify data based on the content and structure of a file, without being limited to a specific data classifier.
